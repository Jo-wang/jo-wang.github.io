Zixin Wang

Postdoctoral Research Fellow

CIRES, School of EECS, The University of Queensland, Australia

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow (supervised by Prof. Shazia Sadiq) at CIRES, The University of Queensland, Australia. My research focuses on domain adaptation, test-time adaptation for vision tasks, and vision-language models. I also have experience in graph neural networks (GNNs), molecular property prediction, and diffusion models.

I was supervised by A/Prof. Sen Wang, Dr. Yadan Luo, and Prof. Helen Huang during PhD. I received my Master of IT from The University of Queensland in 2021.
